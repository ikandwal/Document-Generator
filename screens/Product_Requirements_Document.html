Introduction & Overview** This Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines the scope, features, and goals for the new "TaskFlow Premium" subscription tier. This initiative aims to address a growing demand from our power users and larger organizational clients for more sophisticated project management tools, while simultaneously boosting TaskFlow's Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) and competitive positioning. The Premium tier will introduce advanced features such as AI-powered insights, deep analytics, enhanced automation, and enterprise-grade security, positioning TaskFlow as a viable solution for complex project environments. ### **2. Goals** #### **2.1. Business Goals** * **Increase ARPU:** Achieve a 15% increase in overall ARPU within 12 months post-launch. * **Boost Revenue:** Secure 1,000 new Premium tier subscriptions within the first year. * **Reduce Churn:** Decrease Pro tier churn rate by 5% over 6 months by offering a clear upgrade path and retaining high-value users. * **Market Competitiveness:** Position TaskFlow as a leading solution for advanced project management, matching or exceeding competitor offerings in key areas. * **Expand Market Share:** Attract new larger organizational clients who previously found TaskFlow insufficient. #### **2.2. User Goals** * **Enhanced Productivity:** Enable users to manage complex projects more efficiently with advanced tools and automation. * **Improved Decision Making:** Provide deeper insights and predictive analytics to identify risks and optimize resource allocation. * **Greater Control & Customization:** Offer more granular control over workflows, access, and reporting to meet specific team and organizational needs. * **Increased Confidence:** Provide enterprise-grade security and compliance features, fostering trust in TaskFlow for sensitive projects. * **Strategic Oversight:** Empower leaders to gain a higher-level view of project health and portfolio performance. ### **3. Problem Statement** TaskFlow's existing Free and Pro tiers no longer adequately serve the evolving needs of power users, larger teams, and enterprises. These segments require advanced features for complex project planning, resource optimization, risk prediction, and compliance that are currently absent. This feature gap is leading to user churn, hindering ARPU growth, and weakening TaskFlow's competitive standing against more feature-rich alternatives. ### **4. Target Audience** * **Mid-to-Large Businesses:** Organizations with 50+ employees seeking a robust project management solution. * **Enterprise Teams:** Specific departments or divisions within large corporations with complex project requirements. * **Power Users:** Experienced project managers, team leads, and program managers who need sophisticated tools beyond basic task tracking. * **Regulated Industries:** Companies in sectors with strict compliance and audit requirements (e.g., finance, healthcare, government). ### **5. Proposed Solution: TaskFlow Premium Tier Features** The TaskFlow Premium tier will include all features of the Pro tier, plus the following: #### **5.1. AI-Powered Project Insights & Risk Prediction** * **Description:** An AI engine that analyzes project data (deadlines, task dependencies, team workload, past project performance) to proactively identify potential bottlenecks, delays, and resource overloads. * **User Stories:** * "As a project manager, I want AI to alert me when a project is at risk of missing its deadline, so I can intervene early." * "As a team lead, I want to see which team members are consistently overloaded, so I can reallocate tasks fairly." * "As a stakeholder, I want a quick AI-generated summary of project health and potential risks for high-level oversight." #### **5.2. Advanced Custom Workflow Automation** * **Description:** A powerful, visual workflow builder allowing users to create multi-step, conditional automation rules beyond simple task triggers. * **User Stories:** * "As a team lead, I want to automatically assign a QA review task to a specific team member once a development task is marked 'complete'." * "As an operations manager, I want to set up an approval workflow that requires sign-off from two different department heads before a project can move to the next stage." * "As an administrator, I want to define global workflow templates that my teams can easily adapt." #### **5.3. Enhanced Reporting & Business Intelligence Dashboard** * **Description:** Customizable dashboards with widgets for key performance indicators (KPIs), burn-down/up charts, portfolio overview, resource utilization, and the ability to export data in multiple formats (PDF, CSV, Excel) with advanced filtering. * **User Stories:** * "As a director, I want a single dashboard view of the status, budget, and risks across all projects in my portfolio." * "As a project manager, I want to create a custom report showing task completion rates per team member over the last quarter." * "As a finance team member, I need to export all project time logs for billing purposes, filtered by client and date range." #### **5.4. Enterprise-Grade Security & Compliance** * **Description:** Granular role-based access control (RBAC), audit logs for all actions, single sign-on (SSO) integration (SAML/OAuth), and compliance certifications (e.g., SOC 2, ISO 27001). * **User Stories:** * "As an IT administrator, I want to restrict certain project views or modification capabilities based on a user's department or security clearance." * "As a compliance officer, I need access to a comprehensive audit trail of all project changes, user logins, and data exports." * "As an enterprise customer, I require TaskFlow to integrate with our existing Okta SSO solution." #### **5.5. Dedicated Account Management & Priority-Plus Support** * **Description:** Each Premium client will be assigned a dedicated account manager for onboarding, strategic guidance, and proactive check-ins. Support tickets from Premium users will receive highest priority with guaranteed faster response times. * **User Stories:** * "As a new Premium customer, I want a dedicated point of contact to help my team set up TaskFlow effectively." * "As a project manager facing an urgent issue, I expect immediate support and resolution for my critical problem." * "As a business owner, I want regular check-ins with my account manager to ensure we're maximizing our investment in TaskFlow." ### **6. Pricing Strategy** * **TaskFlow Premium:** $25/user/month (or $250/user/year) * **Enterprise Tier (Future Consideration):** For organizations with 500+ users, potential custom pricing with additional features (e.g., on-premise deployment, dedicated infrastructure). ### **7.
